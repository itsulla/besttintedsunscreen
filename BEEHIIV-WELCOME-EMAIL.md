# Beehiiv Welcome Email — ready to paste

This is the welcome email that goes out **the moment someone subscribes**
to your newsletter. It doubles as your first affiliate revenue opportunity
since every product mention is a tracked Amazon link.

---

## How to set this up (5 minutes)

1. Log in to Beehiiv → your publication
2. Navigate to **Automations → + New automation**
3. Choose template: **"Welcome new subscribers"**
4. Trigger: **When subscriber is added** (default)
5. Delay: **Immediately**
6. Action: **Send email** — click it to open the editor
7. Paste the **Subject line** and **Body** below into the Beehiiv editor
8. Send yourself a test → verify links work → publish the automation

## Design notes for Beehiiv editor

- **Keep all three product blocks** — they're the revenue hooks
- **Don't add a hero image** — Gmail clips image-heavy welcome emails into
  the "Promotions" tab
- **Test on mobile** — most email opens are mobile; Beehiiv's preview has a
  phone toggle in the top right
- **Use Beehiiv's "Text" blocks** for everything except the product sections;
  use **"Button" blocks** for the three "Buy on Amazon" CTAs so they render
  consistently across email clients
- **Merge tag for first name** — Beehiiv's variable is `{{ subscriber.first_name }}`
  or similar depending on their editor version. If you don't collect first names
  (the current form doesn't), change "[First Name]" to just "Hi,"

## After it's live: the 3-sale minimum

Amazon Associates requires **3 qualifying sales within 180 days** of your
first tracked click, or your account gets closed.

This welcome email is literally designed to generate those 3 sales from
your earliest subscribers. It's the single highest-ROI thing you can set up
right now — so please don't skip it.

Once the automation is live and 5-10 people have subscribed, check
**Amazon Associates → Reports → Earnings** to see if clicks are converting.
Adjust the email based on what you learn.
